What we'll build together

Three calm stages. Not a hundred scary tasks.

The work below is deep — but you'll never feel its weight. We move one stage at a time, together, at the pace of clarity.

01

Foundation

So you exist — and look real

Google Business Profile

Claimed, verified, right category, photos, services, hours, booking link. This is what surfaces you on "pranic healing near me."

WhatsApp Business

A click-to-chat link, a tidy catalogue of sessions, quick replies and labels — so no enquiry ever slips away.

Your own website

A clean, mobile-first home on your own name. Who you help, your story, gentle "what is pranic healing", and one obvious way to book.

Professional email

you@yourname instead of a random Gmail. A small thing that quietly tells people you're the real, serious thing.

02

Findability & Trust

So you're discovered — and believed

Local SEO basics

One consistent name, address & phone everywhere, the right local keywords, and schema so Google truly understands your practice.

Directory listings

Justdial, Sulekha, Practo and trusted wellness directories — wherever seekers in your city are already looking.

A gentle review engine

A one-tap way to invite Google reviews (a QR + WhatsApp link), and a simple habit of replying. Trust, made visible.

Instagram, set up right

A searchable name, a bio that says who you help and the outcome, business tools, and Highlights that work like a mini-website.

One link hub

A single bio link that quietly leads to everything — book, message, learn, review — with no confusion.

Trust signals

SSL, fast load, real photos, clear FAQs. The unglamorous details that make a first-time visitor feel safe.

03

Growth

So presence becomes booked sessions

A sustainable content rhythm

Simple, repeatable pillars — demystify the practice, share transformations, show the human behind the healing. Batched so it never drains you.

Booking & payments, simplified

A scheduler or WhatsApp flow plus UPI / Razorpay links — so booking you and paying you is effortless and never awkward.

A gentle invitation

A free guided meditation or short intro PDF, so curious souls can raise their hand before they commit.

Light, kind analytics

Just enough insight to see what's working — without drowning you in dashboards you'll never open.
